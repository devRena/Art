Platforms Project@Art-Athina 2016 Curated by Artemis Potamianou

Platforms Project @ Art Athina was started in 2013 with the aim of charting artistic action as it is generated by the collective initiatives of artists who decide to create the so-called platforms and jointly pursue the answers to their artistic concerns.

Art-Athina Contemporaries 2016: Tribute to george Lappas Curator: Katerina Koskina

Art Athina 2016 in collaboration with the Athens School of Fine Arts [ASFA] decided to pay tribute to the artist and professor of sculpture George Lappas, whose early death left a void in the realm of art.

Honorary Collection 2016: The collection of the Municipal Art Gallery of Athens curated by Denis Zacharopoulos

The Municipal Art Gallery of Athens is key to the study of Greece's artistic, cultural and political history. It was founded during the mayorship of Emmanuel Benakis, a prominent associate of Venizelos and member of the modernisation movement.
